EVENTS

Derek Diluzio Photography

 

Holmes Brothers Farm is a small, family-operated, sustainable farm in the heart of Western NC, just 25 minutes outside of Asheville. We are nestled into a holler with a view of mountains, a field of hops, and farm animals grazing. We offer our custom timber frame pavilion with a fire pit and plenty of flat pasture for you to hold your event. We are happy to help you coordinate your event, raise food for your meals, and grow flowers for your special day. We host outdoor only events from May to October.

We are putting a pause on weddings for 2022 to make our venue even more magical. Check back with us to book weddings in 2023!
